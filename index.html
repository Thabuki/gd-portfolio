About

Originally from Rio de Janeiro, Brazil, I first discovered games through Doom II, where using commands like IDCLIP showed me how they really worked. As a teenager, I dabbled in modding just for fun, never imagining it could become a career.

I later spent six years in Canada studying and refining my skills in game design, level design, and programming. With a background in graphic design, I bring a creative perspective to both gameplay and teamwork.

Now, after 6 years, back in Brazil, I'm looking for new opportunities to keep learning, push boundaries, and contribute to the ever-evolving world of games.

Games unite people.

Everyone should play.
