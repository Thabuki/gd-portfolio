About

Originally from Rio de Janeiro, Brazil, I first discovered my passion for games as a child playing Doom II, where experimenting with commands like idclip opened my eyes to how games truly worked.

That spark of curiosity set me on a lifelong journey into design and creation.

I later moved to Canada to embark on a quest for knowledge, deepening my expertise in game design, level design, and programming.

My academic background in graphic design adds a fresh perspective to everything I do—whether it's shaping gameplay, building immersive worlds, or supporting a team through creative challenges.

Now back in Brazil, I'm always looking for new and challenging opportunities to push boundaries and contribute to the ever-evolving realms of the video game industry.

Games unite people.

Everyone should play.
